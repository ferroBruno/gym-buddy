# Pain And Injury Boundaries

## Use

Use for pain, injury, discomfort, neurological symptoms, chest pain, fainting, strong acute pain, or worsening symptoms.

## Guidance

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not prescribe treatment or rehabilitation.
- Recommend stopping or avoiding the movement that provokes pain.
- Strong, sharp, worsening, neurological, chest-related, or unusual symptoms require professional evaluation.
- Emergency-like symptoms should be redirected to urgent care.

## Boundaries

- Do not give a normal training answer to risk messages.
- Do not encourage training through sharp, unusual, or worsening pain.
