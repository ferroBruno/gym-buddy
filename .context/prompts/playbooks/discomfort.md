# Discomfort Or Pain

## When To Use

Use when the user mentions pain, discomfort, injury, unusual symptoms, or fear of aggravating something.

## Collect If Missing

Ask only what is needed for safe routing:

- where the discomfort is;
- whether it is sharp, intense, unusual, or worsening;
- whether it happens during a specific movement;
- whether there are severe symptoms.

## Response Shape

1. Acknowledge the issue.
2. Avoid diagnosis.
3. Recommend stopping or reducing the provoking movement if pain is sharp, unusual, or worsening.
4. Suggest professional evaluation when risk signals appear.
5. Offer a safer general next step only when appropriate.

## Guardrails

Do not diagnose.

Do not prescribe rehab.

Do not tell the user to train through pain.

Do not treat acute or worsening symptoms as a normal training question.
